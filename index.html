Biography

Don Norman is a pioneer in the field of Human–Computer Interaction. His work focuses on user-centered design, usability, and the relationship between human cognition and interactive systems.

Relevance to Human–Computer Interaction

Norman’s action cycle and the concepts of the gulfs of execution and evaluation provide a theoretical foundation for understanding how users interact with systems and where usability problems arise.
